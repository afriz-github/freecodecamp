Ir. Soekarno

The Proclaimer of Indonesian Independence

Timeline of Achievements

  • 1901 - Born in Surabaya, East Java
  • 1926 - Graduated as an architectural engineer from Bandung Institute of Technology
  • 1945 - Proclaimed Indonesian Independence and became first President
  • 1955 - Hosted the historic Asia-Africa Conference in Bandung
  • 1960 - Initiated construction of iconic national monuments
  • 1970 - Passed away in Jakarta at age 69
"Give me 1000 old men, and I will remove a mountain; give me 10 young men, and I will shake the world." - Ir. Soekarno
